Voter ID Card Application

The Voter ID Card, officially known as the Electors Photo Identity Card (EPIC), is a photo identity document issued by the Election Commission of India (ECI) to every eligible citizen. Its primary purpose is to serve as proof of identity for Indian citizens while casting their ballot in municipal, state, and national elections.

Brief Description â€“ What is it?

The Voter ID Card is a secure document containing the voter's photograph, name, parent/spouse name, age, gender, and unique EPIC number. It prevents impersonation and facilitates accurate voter identification at polling booths. While essential for voting, it's also widely accepted as valid proof of identity and residence for various official purposes.

Required Documents

Proof of Identity (any one):

  • Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card
  • Driving License
  • Passport
  • Bank Passbook with photo

Proof of Address (any one):

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Passport
  • Utility bills (<1 year old)
  • Bank/Post Office Passbook
  • Rent Agreement

Proof of Age (any one):

  • Birth Certificate
  • 10th Standard Certificate
  • Aadhaar/PAN with DoB
  • Passport/Driving License

Photograph: One recent passport-sized color photo (uploaded online or taken at ERO office)

Eligibility Criteria

  • Citizenship: Must be an Indian citizen
  • Age: 18+ years by January 1st of the revision year
  • Residency: Ordinary resident of the constituency
  • No Disqualifications: Not barred by law (unsound mind, certain convictions)
  • Single Registration: Cannot be enrolled in multiple constituencies

Application Procedure

Online Method (Recommended):

  1. Visit Voters' Service Portal
  2. Register/Login and select Form 6
  3. Fill personal, address, and family details
  4. Upload documents and photograph
  5. Submit and note reference number

Offline Method:

  1. Obtain Form 6 from ERO office/BLO/Post Office
  2. Fill form and attach self-attested copies
  3. Submit to ERO office/BLO
  4. Get acknowledgment receipt

Post-Submission Process:

  • Field verification by Booth Level Officer
  • Public scrutiny period
  • Track status using reference number
  • Receive e-EPIC (downloadable) and physical card by post

Trusted Websites

FAQs & Important Tips

Q: What is the "Qualifying Date"?
A: January 1st of the revision year - you must be 18+ by this date to enroll that year.
Q: How to check my name in voter list?
A: Search on Voters' Service Portal using name, relative's name or EPIC number.
Q: What if I move constituencies?
A: Submit Form 6 for new constituency and request deletion from old one.
Voter's Checklist:
  • Apply well before elections (don't wait for announcements)
  • Download e-EPIC as soon as approved
  • Participate in special revision drives when announced
  • Consider voluntary Aadhaar linking via Form 6B
  • Update details promptly if you change address
  • Physical card may take 15-30 days after approval
